    • Rise of a First Lady

    Hillary Clinton is the Democratic nominee for the 2016 election. She was once known as the First Lady, when her husband, Bill Clinton, was president from 1993 to 2001. Hillary was raised in the small town of Park Ridge, Illinois. Clinton was elected in 2000 as the first female senator from New York and hopefully one day will as well be the first woman in the Oval Office. Also in July of 2016, Hillary formally accepted her party's nomination.
