    • Donald Trump, Biography of a Business Man

    Donald John Trump, born June 14th 1968, is one of the primary candidates for the 58th presidential election. He is also a reality TV star, hosting the long running show, The Apprentice. Trump is the chairman of the Trump Organization. Through his organization, Trump has built skyscrapers, hotels, casinos, golf courses and a multitude of other developments across the world. In June of 2015, he announced his candidacy for president as a Republican, and quickly became the front-runner. In July of 2016, he was officially nominated for president.
