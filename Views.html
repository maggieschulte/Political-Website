    Issue Donald Trump Hillary Clinton
    Immigration He wants to enforce immigrations laws, and build a wall. Send criminal and illegal immigrants home, but if aliens go through the correct process, allow them back into the US,legally. Wants to keep families together and help more eligible people become naturalized.
    Abortion He is pro-life. He's opposed to abortion, except in cases of rape, incest, and the life of the mother. He opposes the the use of government funds to pay for abortions. Large supporter of Planned Parenthood, and will stand up for women's access to critical health services, including safe, legal abortion.
    Gun Control Supports the Second Amendment, to bear arms, for protection. However, prosecute violent criminals, and get gangs and drug dealers off the streets. He wants to expand mental health facilities to keep the violent mentally ill off the streets. Will fight for reforms about who can own a gun to keep them away from terrorists, domestic abusers, and other violent criminals. She will include background checks, and will close loopholes that allow gun to fall into the wrong hands.
    Foreign Policy Focused on destroyed radical Islamic terrorist groups. Wants to end the nuclear deal with Iran. America will stand will its allies, and will stop apologizing to its enemies. Her top priority is the safety of America, and to defend our values. Wants to maintain a strong military, strengthen our alliances, being firm with rivals, defeating ISIS, and enforcing the Iran nuclear deal.
    Taxes "Cut business rate to 15 percent. Reduce individual rates to three brackets of 12, 25, and 33 percent, with a 0 percent rate for many. Add above-the-line deduction for childcare costs, including for stay-at-home parents." She's going to make sure that the wealthy don't pay less than the lower and middle class families and will give tax relief to working families.
    Gay Marriage States that same-sex marriage should be decided by the states. She'll fight for equality of the LGBT community under the law, and to end discrimination in employment, schools, and other aspect of society.
    Health Care Wants to repeal Obamacare. Wants a patient-centered health care system, so families and doctors can be the number one decisions makers. Lastly, wants to provide the sale of health insurance across state lines. Defends the Affordable Care Act and will build off of it, and reduce costs. Will crack down on drug companies charging extensive prices and provide new credit to those facing high health expenses.
    Economy and Jobs Wants to reform the tax code and trade policies to make it easier to invest, hire, build, grow, and manufacture in the US. Trying to make America the best place to do business. Cut taxes for the middle class, raise the minimum wage, and ensure that the wealthy pay fairly. Invest in infrastructure, clean energy, and education. She will also help parents struggling with work and family.
    Crime and Safety Wants to create a strong, swift fair law and order. America must work with law enforcement and first responders for the safety of our communities. Need to end mass incarceration, use strategies like police body cameras, increase substance abuse treatment, and aim resources at criminals who pose the greatest threat. Need to invest in education and job training.
    Education Every child deserves a great education. He will enhance education options for students and respect those who are homeschooled. He would make college more affordable, so families aren't buried in debt. Every child deserves high-class education. Need to strike the balance on testing and must provide teachers with the training and resources they need.
    Civil Liberties Increase in anti-terror surveillance with court controlled access, and scrutinize social media of those seeking to enter to US, and limit the ability of groups, like ISIS to use the internet to spread terrorism. Wants to work on discovering and disrupting terrorist plots before they can occur. Supports the USA Freedom Act, Obama signed, that protects privacy, as well as giving law enforcement what they need to keep us safe.
    National Security To ensure our protection and peace, we must strengthen America. We must rebuild our military, reform our intelligence agencies, and prepare our government for the security challenges of cyberspace. Wants to maintain strong military and work with our allies to defeat ISIS. Also wants to launch an intelligence surge, and keep guns away from suspected terrorists.
